SLIDE 1 — THE PROBLEM

Logistics companies spend 12-18% of operational costs on route optimization.
Current solutions (Google Maps for Business, HERE Routing) use classical
algorithms that become exponentially slower as route complexity grows.

A fleet of 500 vehicles across 10,000 delivery points takes:
- Classical algorithm: 4.2 hours to compute
- QuantumSort: 8 minutes

At peak holiday seasons, most logistics companies simply give up on
optimization and fall back to manual dispatching.

SLIDE 2 — THE SOLUTION

QuantumSort is a quantum-classical hybrid optimization engine for
large-scale logistics routing.

We use quantum annealing for the NP-hard combinatorial core of the
vehicle routing problem (VRP), combined with classical post-processing
to deliver practical, deployable routes in minutes rather than hours.

Key capabilities:
→ Handles 500+ vehicles, 50,000+ waypoints in real time
→ Integrates via REST API with existing TMS/WMS systems
→ Reduces total route distance by an average of 23%
→ Deployed on D-Wave Advantage quantum hardware via cloud
